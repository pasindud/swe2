Personal Banking Savings
Regular Savings Accountmore_vert
Regular Savings Accountclose

You can access your account 24 hours x 7 days a week through our worldwide Cirrus and Visa ATM network, Online Banking and Mobile Banking facilities.
 

The Essentials

  • Interest is calculated daily and credited to your account monthly

  • You have the option of choosing a Statement Savings or a Pass Book Savings Account

  • Access your account 24 hours x 7 days a week from any part of the country or even from overseas

 

Eligibility

If you are a resident of Sri Lanka over the age of 18 years, you are eligible to open a Regular Savings Account.

 

Opening an account

You can open either a Statement Savings or a Pass Book Savings Account according to your choice. This could be as an individual account in your name or even as a joint account with one or more parties. Whatever the type of account you choose, you will also receive an ATM cum Debit card.

Your account can be opened at any Commercial Bank branch. Remember to take your national identity card, driving license or passport. This is important for us to identify you and ensure your account’s security.

Apart from cash deposits, you can also deposit cheques, drafts and money orders which are drawn in favour of your name.

Fast, convenient and 24 x 7 unlimited access points to your account from anywhere in the world through Online Banking, Telephone & Mobile Banking or through the millions of ATMs belonging to Cirrus or Visa networks anytime and anywhere.

You can also make payments for goods and services using your ATM cum Debit card, to any merchant with a POS machine connected to Visa or Master network.

 

Making withdrawals

You can make withdrawals from any ATM cum Debit card or over the Bank counter.

If you are a Passbook Savings Account holder, please produce your passbook along with the duly completed withdrawal form, when making over the counter withdrawals.

Children's Accountmore_vert
Children's Accountclose

 

The Essentials

  • A higher interest rate than the rate offered for Regular Savings Accounts

  • Scholarships to the Arunalu Account Holders Who gained the 1st, 2nd and 3rd highest aggregate marks in the country at the Year 5 Scholarship Examination

  • Cash prizes of Rs 10,000/-, Rs 7,500/- and Rs 5,000/- respectively for account holders who have gained the 1st, 2nd and the 3rd  highest marks in each school

Arunalu is the perfect gift for your son, daughter, grandchildren, nephews and nieces

 

Benefits of Arunalu Account

 

Scholarships

Scholarships to the Arunalu Account Holders Who gained the 1st, 2nd and 3rd highest aggregate marks in the country at the Year 5 Scholarship Examination as given below:

    • Island Ranking 1 - A scholarship of  Rs 2,000/-per month

    • Island Ranking 2 - A scholarship of  Rs 1,500/-per month

    • Island Ranking 3 - A scholarship of  Rs 1,000/-per month

Awarding of Scholarships will commence from the month of January of the following year when the student enters Year 6 and will continue until the student reaches 18 years of age.

The three students who are ranked 1st , 2nd and 3rd in the country will not be eligible to the Cash Prizes mentioned below.

There has to be a minimum balance of Rs. 5,000/- in the Arunalu Account on the date of the Examination.

 

Cash prizes

A special cash prize of Rs. 50,000/-will be awarded to the Arunalu Account Holders who scores the highest aggregate marks in each district.

Cash prizes of Rs 10,000/-, Rs 7,500/-and Rs 5,000/-will be awarded to the Arunalu Account Holders who have gained the 1st, 2nd and the 3rd  highest marks in each school at the Year 5 Scholarship Examination.

 

Account opening process

Any child from an infant to 18 years of age is eligible to open an Arunalu Account and obtain benefits from this Children's Savings Account. Interest will be credited monthly to the child’s account.

To open an account please collect an Arunalu Minor's Savings Account form, from your nearest Commercial Bank branch or download it from this link, complete the form and hand it in with the original birth certificate of the child together with a photocopy. The original birth certificate will be returned to you.  Remember to take your national identity card, driving license or passport. This is important for us to identify you and ensure your account’s security. 

 

Withdrawals

The total balance in the Arunalu Children's Savings Account can be withdrawn, on and after the 18th birthday of the child. It can also be transferred to one of our other deposit products such as Regular Savings Account, Power Savings Account, Super Saver Account or a Fixed Deposit.
