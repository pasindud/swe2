Help FAQ
Loginmore_vert
Loginclose

You can click on the 'Online Banking' from the menu bar and then you will be directed to the login screen. There you can give your registered username and password and loginto view your accounts.

You may be experiencing difficulties accessing your account due to one of the following reasons:
• The username and/or password you have entered is incorrect
• You have had three invalid login attempts and have been locked out


If you have verified all of the above information and are still having difficulty accessing your account, please call us at +94 112296577.

Securitymore_vert
Securityclose

Online Banking uses industry-standard security protocols that leverage encryption for transferring data. Encryption helps create a secure environment for the information being transferred between your browser and Bank.

These security protocols help protect data in 3 key ways:

  • Authentication ensures that you are communicating with us and prevents another computer from impersonating Bank.
  • Encryption scrambles transferred data so that it cannot be read by unauthorized parties.
  • Data integrity, in terms of data and network security, assures that information can only be accessed and modified by those authorized to do so.
Pay billsmore_vert
Pay billsclose

You can go to 'Pay Bills' under 'Payments'. You can select a 'Merchant service' among the listed merchant accounts and 'Paying account' from your registered accounts. Then you can simply give the Bill reference number and Amount to pay the bill.

Transaction Historymore_vert
Transaction Historyclose

You can view your 'Transaction History' under the 'Account' section. You can view transaction data for a selected date range.
