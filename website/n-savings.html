Personal Banking Savings

You may open this account with the Bank while you are employed abroad or within 90 days of your return to Sri Lanka. Sri Lankan migrants can also invest in NRFC accounts.

 

The Essentials

  • Accounts can be opened with US Dollars, Sterling Pounds, Swiss Francs, Japanese Yen, Australian Dollars, Canadian Dollars, Hong Kong Dollars, Euros, Singapore Dollars, New Zealand Dollars, and Chinese Yuan, Swedish Kroners, Norwegian Kroners

  • Interest calculated daily, and credited to your account quarterly enabling you to earn much more than published rates

  • Interest earned is tax free even after you come to Sri Lanka

  • Funds are freely transferable without being subject to the securities and exchange commission regulations

 

Benefits of NRFC Savings Accounts

You can apply for Sri Lanka Rupee Loans or Overdrafts at concessional rates. This facility enables you to retain the foreign currency deposits intact.

NRFC Accounts can be freely operated even after your return to the island for permanent residence.

Arrangements can be made for a member of your family or friend to operate this account on your behalf.

You will receive the option of appointing a nominee, to whom the funds in the account are to be paid, in the event of death of the account holder.

Standing orders are effected without any commission.

You can switch from one currency to another at competitive exchange rates.

Above all you will receive our personalized service.

 

Opening a NRFC Account

You can visit a branch of your choice and discuss your requirement along with the period of deposit with an officer of the branch.

Interest rate applicable for Foreign Currency Savings can be viewed from the Rates and Tariffs page of this site.

Please fill the relevant application form and hand over to the branch. Remember to take your passport with the VISA copy to confirm your non residency status. This is necessary for us to identify you.

Interest

Interest will be calculated on the daily balance and credited to your account quarterly. The interest earned on NRFC Accounts is free of all taxes even after your return to Sri Lanka. NRFC funds are freely transferable. (in accordance with the Exchange Control Regulations)

 

Deposits

All types of inward remittances in foreign currency, as given below, will be accepted in credit to NRFC accounts.

• Telegraphic or SWIFT transfers
• Bank Drafts drawn in favor of Commercial Bank of Ceylon PLC
• USD Drafts payable at local Banks or issued by local Banks. Credit on which will be given within 4 working    days without any charge
• Foreign currency notes
• Traveller's cheques  issued overseas and personal cheques
• Bank Drafts drawn on foreign currency in the currency of the respective countries
   eg: USD drafts drawn on New York,  GBP drafts drawn on London

 

Withdrawals

You can make withdrawals from NRFC accounts on the following basis.

•  Calling over personally and on production of your passport or national identity card
•  Through any ATM displaying cirrus or visa logo in the currency of the country
•  On your written instructions to pay another party; eg. For the upkeep of your family
•  Written instructions for the purpose of remitting abroad in any foreign currency
•  For obtaining traveller's cheques and currency notes for travel abroad
